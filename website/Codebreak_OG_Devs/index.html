Our vision
"Global hunger won't be stopped globaly until we start with oneself"

Around 1.3 billion tonnes of food is wasted annually, which is equivalent to more than half of the world's total cereal production and when this food is disposed off in landfills, it produces harmful greenhouse gases and climate change. If this food wastage keeps on increasing then in near future there will be huge shortage of food. To solve this we have come up with a solution through our app !Perish. !Perish lists down your groceries for you so you don't forget them and reminds you to use them before they expire.We also have an option for you to donate the food you have left as we are linked with NGO's so nor does your food get wasted plus the food gets sent to the people in need. We also provide you with delicious recipes that you can make with the items that are about to expire.This solves both food wastage and global hunger.Alligned with NGO's we strive to build a world where no one is left to sleep with an empty stomach.
